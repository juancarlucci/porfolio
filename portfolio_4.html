About

I am a mix of various cultures and perspectives. Born and raised in balmy Guatemala, my formative years were an immersion into brilliant contrast of textures, emotions and experiences: civil strife, poverty, wealth; tropical rain forests and madeningly polluted urban environments. My early teen years placed my family and I in the small town of Ashland, Oregon, where puberty developed against a backdrop of Shakespearean plays, small-town politics, identity-crisis, and deep friendships. From my late 20s to the present the Bay Area of SF has been the platform for my development in career paths, personal journeys and deepening community ties.
